Storages - List

Storages

Residence: Root storage, no parent.
Room: Can have Residence as parent, may include Furniture, Units, and Items directly.
Furniture: Can have Residence or Room as parent, may include Units and Items.
Unit: Can have any storage as parent, may include only Items.

Items

Items can be stored in any storage type.
Items may have keywords for easier searching.
